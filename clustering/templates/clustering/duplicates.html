Help — How are possible duplicates found?

A scan compares every pair of embedded tickets in the project. A pair is only flagged if all three hold: cosine similarity ≥92% (a much higher bar than clustering — this is about near-identical tickets, not "same topic"), created within 7 days of each other, and reported by the same person or against the same application. High similarity alone isn't enough — two genuinely different-but-related issues can be very similar in embedding space without being duplicates.

Confirm Duplicate / Not a Duplicate only record your review — they don't merge or delete either ticket. Once reviewed, a pair won't reappear in a future scan.
