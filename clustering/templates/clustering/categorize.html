Help — How are queue suggestions made?

For each untriaged ticket (queue "Unspecified"), its embedding is compared against every ticket in the project that already has a real queue value — either mapped from your source data's "Assignment Group"/"Queue" column, or a previously accepted suggestion. The 5 nearest neighbors vote, and the majority queue among them is suggested — the "agreement" percentage is literally how many of those 5 agree, not a hidden confidence score.

No LLM is involved — this is the same cosine-similarity ranking behind Ask Correlate and Find Similar Tickets, applied to majority-vote a label instead of ranking search results.
