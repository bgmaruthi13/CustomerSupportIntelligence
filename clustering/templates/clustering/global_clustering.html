Help — What this page is for

Every other clustering page in this app is scoped to one project on purpose — a team's tickets shouldn't cluster together with an unrelated team's by accident. This page is the deliberate exception: pick two or more projects, run the same Traditional ML or Generative AI engine across their combined tickets, and see what comes out. Results are kept separate from your regular per-project clusters — they live in their own set, only visible here.

The Cross-Project Intersections table is the actual point of this page: a cluster whose members come from more than one selected project means the same underlying issue — a shared vendor, a common third-party API, a company-wide outage — is showing up in multiple teams' ticket queues, something no single project's own clustering could ever reveal on its own.

Running again replaces the previous global run for that engine (same "wipe and rebuild" behavior as the per-project pages) — it doesn't accumulate history across runs.
