Help — Projects (tenants)

Each project is a fully isolated workspace — its own tickets, uploads, clusters, embeddings, and settings. Nothing is shared between projects; switching projects (via the switcher in the top bar, or by clicking a card here) changes what every other page in the app shows.

Use separate projects for genuinely separate ticket populations — different systems, different teams, or a sandbox for trying a new dataset without touching a real one. A SAMPLE DATA badge marks projects seeded with synthetic demo tickets rather than a real upload.
